About OpenKarma

OpenKarma is a new tool to help bridge the gap between developers and designers. The idea came after noticing a huge need for OSS projects to find design help which previously seemed unattainable.

How it will work

Once we’ve launched you’ll be able to log in with your GitHub, Behance, or email address. Then you can connect projects you need design help with, find designers to work with and discover projects to collaborate on.

Want to get involved?

We have a good amount of OSS devs already excited to participate which is awesome. We really need more outreach in the design community. If you’re a designer or developer looking to keep up to date with OpenKarma, sign up for our updates newsletter.
