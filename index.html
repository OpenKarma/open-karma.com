About Open Karma

Open Karma is a new tool to help bridge the gap between developers and designers. The idea came after noticing a huge need for OSS projects to find design help which previously seemed unattainable.

How it will work

Once we’ve launched you’ll be able to log in with your GitHub, Behance, or email. Then you can connect projects you need design help with, find designers to work with and discover projects to collaborate on.

Want to get involved?

We're already receiving great interest— but, we want more! If you’re a designer or developer looking to keep up to date with Open Karma please sign up for our updates.
